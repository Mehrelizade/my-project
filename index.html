What Is a Big Hairy Audacious Goal (BHAG) and How Does It Serve?

It's time for aspiring entrepreneurs and students to embark on a journey towards envisioning and kickstarting the success path of a company or organization in the eyes of stakeholders and learners. This journey commences with acquainting oneself with the concept of a "Big Hairy Audacious Goal" (BHAG) and attempting to understand how this ambitious objective can be a powerful driver in an organization's journey to success.

What Is BHAG?

BHAG is a goal created for independent thinking and differentiation. Coined in the book "Built to Last: Successful Habits of Visionary Companies" by James Collins and Jerry Porras, the fundamental essence of this terminology is to set the long-term goals of an organization with the purpose of ensuring its development, attracting employees, and making strategic decisions. BHAG is typically defined for a period of 10-25 years and is utilized to guide the overall development of the organization.

Why Is BHAG Important?

BHAG provides a single core focus and an audacious goal for an organization. This goal aids in creating unity and excitement among employees, establishing long-term pathways, and forming a collective sense of purpose within the organization's journey to success.

Examples of BHAGS

Let's examine some international BHAG examples established by globally recognized organizations throughout history:

  • NASA's objective of sending humans to the moon and safely bringing them back to Earth.
  • Google's mission to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.
  • Amazon's ambition to become the world's most customer-centric company.

Creating a BHAG for Your Organization:

Readers of this article can begin approaching the creation and implementation of their own BHAGs for their respective organizations. Whether it's a local business, a major corporation, or even a firefighting brigade, setting a BHAG can help pave a new route to success and achieve substantial growth.

Conclusion:

BHAG serves as a potent tool for organizations to set and accomplish ambitious goals and strategies. It plays a pivotal role in defining, developing, and executing the visionary objectives of an organization, while motivating employees through an inspiring mission.
